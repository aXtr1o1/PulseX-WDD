Where the sea meets the mountains, your closest seaside escape begins.
A retreat that brings the coast closer than ever, inviting you to pause, 
breathe, and embrace life by the water.
Founded in 2005, Wadi Degla Developments — a subsidiary of Wadi Degla Holding — stands as one of Egypt’s 
leading real estate developers, with a total development footprint of 6 million square meters. Over the past 
20 years, we’ve delivered more than 18,000 units across 17 projects in Cairo, Giza, the North Coast, and Ain 
Sokhna.
Driven by a vision to redeﬁne contemporary living, we create communities that combine high-end, smart 
infrastructure, and thoughtful planning — tailored to the evolving needs of Egyptian families. Every 
development is built with care, integrating green spaces, essential services, and a lasting sense of comfort 
and quality.
On the Red Sea coast, our portfolio includes six projects spanning 2.1 million square meters — featuring over 
6,000 residential units and two premium beachfront hotels — offering a distinctive coastal lifestyle that 
blends exclusivity with ease.
On the Mediterranean, our North Coast developments bring together prime locations, reﬁned design, and a 
vibrant summer energy — offering residents and visitors a seamless blend of serenity and sophistication.

More than a place to live, Ojo is a destination shaped around balance, where 
thoughtfully designed spaces bring people together naturally. Overlooking the water
